Mwesel

Site Plan

Mwesel means "eat" in Pingelapese. This name captures the core purpose of the app—helping people decide what to eat by offering recipe ideas in a fun, convenient way. It's simple, cultural, and relevant.

Site Purpose

Mwesel is a recipe app designed to help users quickly discover what to cook. It generates random recipes from TheMealDB API and allows users to save their favorites in localStorage. It’s especially helpful for couples or individuals who often struggle with deciding what to make for meals. The app is mobile-first and built for simplicity and speed.

Scenarios

  • What should we cook for dinner tonight?
  • Can I save a recipe to cook again later?
  • Are there fun themes like taco tuesdays or throwback thursdays?
