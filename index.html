Pohnpei, FSM

Pohnpei is one of the four states of the Federated States of Micronesia, located in the western Pacific Ocean. Known for its lush rainforests, waterfalls, and ancient ruins like Nan Madol, it's often called the “Garden Island” of Micronesia. The island has a rich cultural heritage, with deep-rooted traditions and a strong sense of community. While it faces modern challenges like limited infrastructure, Pohnpei remains a place of natural beauty and cultural pride.
